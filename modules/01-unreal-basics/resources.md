## Fab Marketplace and Free Asset Packs

### Fab (formerly Unreal Marketplace)
- **URL**: [https://www.fab.com/](https://www.fab.com/)
- Browse and download free and paid assets. Filter by "Free" and search "fantasy" or "medieval" for relevant content.

### Recommended Free Fantasy Packs
These are excellent starting points for a DnD-style project:

- **Infinity Blade: Warriors** and **Infinity Blade: Weapons**: High-quality fantasy characters and weapons from Epic Games. Free.
- **Paragon Character Assets**: Free character models with animations. Useful for placeholder heroes and enemies.
- **Megascans** (via Quixel Bridge, integrated into UE5): Photorealistic scanned materials and props. Free for Unreal Engine projects. Search for stone, wood, and dungeon-relevant surfaces.

### How to Import Fab Assets
1. Add to library on Fab or the Epic Games Launcher.
2. In UE5, open the Content Drawer (or Content Browser).
3. Click **Add > Add Fab Content** and select the asset pack.
4. Wait for the import to complete. Assets appear in your Content Browser under the pack's folder.
